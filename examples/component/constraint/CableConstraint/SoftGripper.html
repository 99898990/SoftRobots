Soft gripper robot with cables controlled by keyboard

The Soft gripper robot is composed of three fingers made of elastic material, these three fingers are actuated with cable.

In this scene we demonstrate the use of the CableConstraint and PythonScriptController components to be able to interactively manipulate the robot.


How to use the demo

After clicking on "Animate" you can click in the 3d view then pulling/releasing the three cables by pressing CTRL+/CTLR-.
